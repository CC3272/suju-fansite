SUPER JUNIOR（슈퍼주니어）是 SM Entertainment 旗下由 9 名成员组成的韩国男子组合，于 2005 年 11 月 6 日出道，被媒体誉为「韩流之王」。凭借 Sorry, Sorry（2009）风靡全球，出道近 20 年仍持续活跃。粉丝名为 E.L.F（Ever Lasting Friends），应援色为珍珠宝蓝。

SUPER JUNIOR is a 9-member South Korean boy group under SM Entertainment, debuting on November 6, 2005. Known as the "Kings of the Hallyu Wave," they rose to global fame with Sorry, Sorry (2009) and remain active after nearly 20 years. Fandom: E.L.F (Ever Lasting Friends). Fan color: Pearl Sapphire Blue.
